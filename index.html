Stage Distribution (Count of entries per pipeline stage)
What each stage means ▸
Initial outreach / Initial Conversation First contact has been made with the donor.
Concept Note A short summary of the proposed project has been shared with the donor.
Proposal A full proposal has been submitted to the donor.
MoU (incl. MoU WIP) Memorandum of Understanding — the near-final agreement before the grant. 'WIP' means it is still being drafted.
Grant Awarded The donor has confirmed the grant.
Project on Hold Paused for now; may resume later.
Lost opportunity Did not proceed.
