Stage Distribution

What each stage means ▸
Initial outreach First contact has been made with the donor.
Initial Conversation/ Pitching Application submitted to the donor — incl. where a donor field visit is still pending.
Concept Note Concept note submitted and under donor review.
Proposal Full proposal submitted and under donor review — incl. where a field visit is pending.
MOU MoU in progress or signed — covers MoU WIP, MoU Signed–WIP and MoU Signed.
Grant Awarded Grant awarded and funds realised.
Project on Hold Paused for now; may resume later.
Lost opportunity Did not proceed.

The earlier combined table has been split into two — Grant Expiry In Next (3 Month) and Utilization Less Than 75% — so each shows grants matching its own condition.

For the Utilization Less Than 75% table: in the early months of any grant, spending has not yet ramped up, so utilization will naturally be below 75% for almost every active grant. The table would then show most grants by default, which does not help highlight the ones that actually need attention.

Please confirm when a grant should appear in this table — for example, only when it is close to its end date, or once a certain percentage of the grant period has passed.
