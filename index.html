Stage Distribution

Count of proposals per pipeline stage — click a bar to drill in
What each stage means ▸
Initial Conversation First discussion with the donor — no formal submission yet.
Initial outreach First contact has been made with the donor.
Concept Note A short summary of the proposed project has been shared with the donor.
Proposal A full proposal has been submitted to the donor.
MoU WIP Memorandum of Understanding still being drafted — close to a deal.
MoU Signed The MoU has been signed — the near-final agreement before the grant is confirmed.
Grant Awarded The donor has confirmed the grant.
Project on Hold Paused for now; may resume later.
Lost opportunity Did not proceed.
